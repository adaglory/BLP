Stem Clubs

Empower, Motivate and spark Interest.

Our STEM club initiative is designed to empower young girls. It attracts and encourages them to be part of a structured and fun learning journey to discover their own potentials. Club members are engaged in collaborative activities helping them build necessary skills and help them gain a new understanding of career options within the STEM fields. The program also creates a dynamic forward-thinking attitude towards life. The STEM club is an outside school set of activities.

Role Models

Inspire, Connect and Unlock

Girls have a hard time picturing themselves in STEM roles. The gender STEM stereotype needs to be removed. A female role model’s success in STEM can be a source of inspiration to young girls and can be emulated. Listening to females who have attained academic and professional achievements in STEM fields is very important for young girls. The impact of female role models is like the snowball effect, it builds on and on. BLPARW is committed to inspiring girls to pursue STEM careers.
