Programs

Our Featured Programs

Adult Literacy

Helping African women develop and improve their abilities to read and write. Building their self-esteem and self-confidence. We are extending this effort to developing skills to support African women on greater initiatives.

Empowerement

Supporting the African rural woman to build the resources, assets, and capabilities she requires to make better choices and take control of her life and environment. Our goal is a long-term social and economic improvement.

Financial Literacy

Helping women achieve proficiency in personal and business finance, as well as investment decisions. Providing the knowledge and skills required to make appropriate financial decisions, create budget and the ability to track spending

Policy

We educate the African rural woman on where and how to find information and guidelines relevant to their businesses. We also encourage them to seek knowledge on rules and procedures to support their objectives.

Partnership

We encourage joining efforts, leveraging on each other’s abilities and resources to make better outcomes. We connect the African rural women we support with relevant organisations in both public and private sectors.

Health Care

At the core of what we do in ensuring a significantly improved and sustained emotional, mental and physical well-being of Africans. We structure our programs to ensure better primary healthcare and reduced mortality rate.
